Yoga Retreat

1/3/5/7 Day Wellness Retreat with Yoga, Meditation, Sound Therapy and More in Patong, Phuket

Join Aadi Yoga & Sound Healing Centre on this energising yoga retreat in Patong, Phuket, Thailand. You will enjoy a wide variety of yoga, Pilates and meditation classes suitable for all practitioner levels. The program is designed to support a holistic experience and promote a deeper connection with your body, mind, and spirit. Alongside the daily classes, Wonderland Healing Centre offers occasional harmonising workshops.

  • Access to the swimming pool and herbal steam sauna
  • 1/2/4/6 nights accommodation
