Master Deepak / The Founder

Master Deepak’s journey is a remarkable blend of ancient tradition and modern scholarship. Born in the sacred city of Rishikesh, in the Himalayas, India, he hails from a venerable priestly family renowned for its profound dedication to Sanskrit and yoga. For eight generations, his family has been custodians of yoga teachings and Sanskrit scholarship, preserving a legacy that Master Deepak now carries forward with pride and devotion.

From an early age, Deepak was immersed in the spiritual and intellectual heritage of his ancestors. His guru, Mahamandaleshwar Dr. Rameshwar Das, a revered spiritual leader and head of ashrams and saints across India, guided him with wisdom and grace. Dr. Rameshwar Das is also the chief of the Manokamna Siddh Hanuman Mandir, famously known as Bada Hanuman Mandir, located at the sacred Triveni Ghat in Rishikesh. Under such profound mentorship, Deepak deepened his understanding of authentic yoga and spiritual practice.

Despite his rich spiritual background, Master Deepak pursued academic excellence in the modern world, earning many master’s degrees in Yoga, Linguistics, Physics, and International Relations and becoming a linguist in Chinese. He spent many years working in office jobs, gaining valuable life experience and a broader perspective. However, the call of his ancestral roots and his passion for yoga eventually led him to fully embrace the yogic path.

Today, Master Deepak is dedicated to spreading authentic yoga, combining his profound traditional knowledge with his worldly insights. His mission is to share the timeless wisdom of yoga and Sanskrit, enriching the lives of those who seek balance, health, and spiritual growth. Through his teachings, the ancient legacy of his family continues to inspire and transform lives in the modern world.
