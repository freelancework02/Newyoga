200 Hour Yoga Teacher Training in Rishikesh, India

At Aadiyoga Center Phuket, we offer the internationally recognized 200 Hour Yoga Teacher Training Course (TTC) in Rishikesh, India, in collaboration with Rishikesh Yogkulam®. This is a one-month, immersive residential program that provides the knowledge, practice, and certification to teach yoga worldwide.

Course Highlights

  • Yoga Alliance USA Accredited Certification
  • Expert teachers with years of experience
  • Daily practice of Asanas, Pranayama, Meditation & Teaching Methodology
  • Traditional Fire Ceremony (Homa & Yajna) at the start of training
  • Evening Ganga Aarti Experience
  • Comprehensive curriculum combining practice & philosophy
  • Comfortable accommodation & sattvic yogic meals

Course Curriculum

  • Yoga Philosophy & Lifestyle
  • Anatomy & Physiology for Yoga
  • Hatha & Ashtanga Yoga Practice
  • Posture Alignment & Adjustments
  • Pranayama & Breathwork
  • Meditation & Concentration Techniques
  • Mantras & Sanskrit Terminology
  • Teaching Methodology & Practicum
