Our Services

At Aadiyoga Center Phuket, we proudly offer authentic Indian yoga and wellness services. Guided by the esteemed yogi Deepak, we are the first Indian-based yoga and wellness center in Patong, Phuket, dedicated to preserving the true essence of Indian yogic tradition.

Yoga Classes

Embrace the transformative power of Hatha, Ashtanga, and Vinyasa Flow in our daily yoga classes. With guidance from expert Indian yogis, develop strength, flexibility, and a deep, lasting inner peace — suitable for all levels.

Kids Yoga

Foster your child's confidence and creativity through playful, mindful Kids Yoga sessions. Designed to enhance focus, balance, and emotional well-being in a joyful, supportive environment.

Yoga Therapy

Discover healing through personalized Yoga Therapy programs crafted to relieve stress, chronic pain, and anxiety — restoring harmony in your body, mind, and spirit.

Sound Healing

Immerse yourself in soothing vibrations of Tibetan singing bowls and gongs. Our Sound Healing sessions reduce stress, release tension, and rejuvenate your spirit.

Training Courses

Deepen your practice with Yoga Alliance USA certified Training Courses, from 100 to 500 hours, guiding you towards mastery, teaching excellence, and a truly transformative journey.
